Activity: Editing frame position using snap points

Click here to download the activity file.

1: Open an assembly
Note:

This activity requires you download the activity file for the Corner Treatment Options activity. If you have not worked that activity, download the activity file and complete step 1 of the activity.

Open edit05.asm.

2: Reposition a frame using handle points

Edit the definition of Frame_2.

Click the Edit Cross Sections step and then click Deselect.

Select the cross section shown and then click the Accept button.

Go to an Iso view and click the Zoom to Selected Cross Section(s) button to zoom in to the cross section as shown.

On the command bar, click the Define Handle Point button .

On the command bar, turn on all keypoints .

Click the snap point shown.

Frame (1) is now centered and above the sketch element.

3: Close the assembly

Click Finish and close edit05.asm.

Activity: Editing a corner treatment

Click here to download the activity file.

1: Open an assembly
Note:

This activity requires you download the activity file for the Corner Treatment Options activity. If you have not worked that activity, download the activity file and complete step 1 of the activity.

Open edit01.asm.

2: Edit the corner treatment

Click the arrow to display the frame components in Assembly PathFinder.

In PathFinder, right-click Frame_1 and choose Edit Definition.

On the command bar, click the Frame Options button. On the Frame Options dialog box, click the Butt corner treatment, ensure the Preferred Axis is set to the X axis, and then click OK.

3: Close the assembly

Click Finish and close edit01.asm without saving the file.

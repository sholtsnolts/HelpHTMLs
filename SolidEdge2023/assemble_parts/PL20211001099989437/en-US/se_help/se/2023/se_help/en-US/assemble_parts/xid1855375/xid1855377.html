Publish Internal Components command

Use the Tools tab→Assistants group→Publish Internal Components list→Publish Internal Components command to create files on disk for internal components and maintain their links to top level assembly.

After internal components are published, you can use them in downstream applications, for example in a parts list with callouts in draft or in an assembly BOM.

Display the status of item numbers

You can determine the status of Solid Edge Item Numbers in relation to Teamcenter Find Numbers when you are in the Draft or Assembly environments.

  • Do one of the following:

    • In the Draft environment, choose File menu→Manage→Item Number Status.

      This command retrieves Find Numbers from Teamcenter and checks for out-of-date Item Numbers. The results display in the Item Number Status dialog box (Draft).

    • In the Assembly environment, right-click the assembly in PathFinder and choose Item Numbers and then choose one of the following commands.

      Status

      Retrieves Find Numbers from Teamcenter and checks for out-of-date Item Numbers. The results display in the Item Number Status dialog box (Assembly).

      Synchronize

      Updates the Solid Edge Item Numbers with Teamcenter Find Number values.

Use multi-select within Assembly PathFinder

  1. Open an assembly in Solid Edge.

  2. In PathFinder, select multiple indirect documents.

    • To select a sequential list of indirect documents, click the first document you want in the list, hold the Shift key, and click the last document you want added. All documents between the first and last selection are included.

    • To select documents non-sequentially, hold the Ctrl key as you click indirect documents.

    Note:

    The selected indirect documents must have a status of Checked Out To You.

  3. Right-click and select Manage→Upload or Manage→Check In.

    The Manage command is performed on all highlighted documents.

    Note:

    If there are no documents in the select set that are checked out to you, the Manage commands are disabled.

Tip:

To clear a selection, click the document highlighted in PathFinder.

Flip a part in an assembly

  1. In Assembly PathFinder or the graphic window, select the part that has a relationship you want to flip.

  2. On the bottom pane of Assembly PathFinder, select the relationship you want to flip.

  3. On the shortcut menu, click Flip.

    Tip:

    When you flip a mate relationship, it is changed to a planar align relationship.

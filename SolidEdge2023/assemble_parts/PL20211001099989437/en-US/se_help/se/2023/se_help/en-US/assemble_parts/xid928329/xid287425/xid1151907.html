Toggle the display status of assembly components

You can use the Toggle Display button on the status bar to toggle the display status of all components. This is useful for displaying hidden parts without the use of PathFinder.

  • To show hidden components in ghost mode, on the status bar, click the Toggle Display button .

    The hidden components display in a ghosted style.

  • To hide the ghosted components, in the graphics window, click the Exit Toggle Display button.

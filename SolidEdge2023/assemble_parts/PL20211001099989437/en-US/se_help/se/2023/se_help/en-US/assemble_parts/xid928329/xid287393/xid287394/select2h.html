Hide the remaining parts in an assembly

You can use this procedure only when you have in-place activated a part or subassembly from the Assembly environment.

  • Choose View tab→Show group→Hide Previous Level. The remaining parts and subassemblies are hidden.

Tip:

You can display the parts and subassemblies again by choosing Hide Previous Level again.

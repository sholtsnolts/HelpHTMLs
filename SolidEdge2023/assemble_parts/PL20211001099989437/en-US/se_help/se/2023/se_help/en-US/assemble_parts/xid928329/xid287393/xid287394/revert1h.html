Close a part without saving changes

  • On the Home tab in the Close group, choose Revert.

    Note:

    The changes you made to the part document are not saved. You are returned to the assembly document.

Tip:

  • The Revert command is available when you in-place activate a part from an assembly.

  • You can use the Close and Return command on the ribbon to save any changes you made to the part document and return to the assembly.

Disperse parts in a subassembly

  1. In PathFinder, click the subassembly you want to disperse.

  2. Choose Home tab→Modify group→Disperse command .

  3. On the Disperse Assembly dialog box, click Yes to disperse the parts in the subassembly and remove the reference to the existing subassembly.

Surface Area Sensor command bar

Positive Area

Specifies that you want to track the positive area of the surface in the sensor. A positive surface area sensor monitors the total area of the surface. Any "holes" or internal boundaries in a surface are not included in the surface area calculation.

Negative Area

Specifies that you want to track the negative area of the surface in the sensor. A negative surface area sensor monitors the "holes" or internal boundaries in a surface.

Select

Defines the type of faces you can select. You can specify that you want to select individual faces or a chain of faces.

Deselect (x)

Clears the selection.

Accept (check mark)

Accepts the selected faces.

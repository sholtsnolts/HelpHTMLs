Assembly Load/Update Progress dialog box

When opening a large assembly directly in the assembly environment or loading the assembly from Assembly Preview an Assembly Load/Update Progress dialog box displays the three stages of load and update:

  • Load the assembly files

  • Update the assembly

  • Display the assembly

To stop the load and update of the assembly, click the Stop button on the dialog box at any time during the load and update process. The assembly displays based on the state of the load or update when the process stopped. You can then close the file without closing Solid Edge.

Show and hide components in an assembly

  1. Display the PathFinder tab.

  2. On the PathFinder tab, click the check mark box for the components you want to show or hide.

    Note:

    If you double-click the check-mark, PathFinder displays all components and sub-components.

Tip:

  • You can select the components, right-click and use the short-cut menu to show or hide the components.

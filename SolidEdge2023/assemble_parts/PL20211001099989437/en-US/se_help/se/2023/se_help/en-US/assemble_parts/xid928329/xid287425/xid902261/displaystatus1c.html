Display Status command (Assembly environment)

Shows or hides the status information for parts and assemblies listed in Assembly PathFinder. When you select the command, the system automatically updates the status information for each file used in the active assembly document.

When you hover over a file name in Assembly PathFinder, a tooltip displays the current document status, the checkout status, and the revision rule used to open the assembly. If the assembly is managed by Teamcenter, the tooltip also displays the variant rule used to open the document.

When you click Display Status, PathFinder displays:

  • A status icon next to each document name.

  • In green text—The names of documents checked out to you.

  • In orange text—The names of documents checked out to other users.

Apply a material to internal components

  1. Select a single or multiple internal component nodes in PathFinder, right-click and choose Material.

  2. Select a material from Recent Materials, Favorite Materials, or Material Table.

    If you select Material Table, select the material name you want from the left pane of the Material Table dialog box, and then click Apply To Model.

Tip:

  • You also can use Properties Manager to view and assign material to the internal components. From the ribbon, in the Inspect tab→Physical Properties group, select Properties Manager .

  • When you publish internal components, you can review the material assigned to each internal component in the Material column in the Publish Internal Components dialog box. You also can assign a material to any internal component that displays (None) by selecting it from the adjacent Material list.

Transfer parts to an existing assembly

  1. In PathFinder, click the part(s) you want to transfer.

    Note:

    To select more than one element, hold the Ctrl key and click each element.

  2. Choose Home tab→Modify group→Transfer .

  3. On the Transfer to Assembly Level dialog box, click the assembly which you want to transfer the parts.

  4. On the Transfer to Assembly Level dialog box, click OK.

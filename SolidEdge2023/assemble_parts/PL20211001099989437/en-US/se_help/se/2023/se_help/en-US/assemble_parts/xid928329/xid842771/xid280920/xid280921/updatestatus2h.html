Update the status information for documents in an assembly

In a Teamcenter-managed document:

  • In PathFinder, select the document for which you want to obtain updated information.

  • Choose Teamcenter tab→Update group→Update Status Info.

    The action applies to all files if nothing is selected in PathFinder.

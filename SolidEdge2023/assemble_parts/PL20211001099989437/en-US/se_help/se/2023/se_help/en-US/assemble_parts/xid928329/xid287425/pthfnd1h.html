Select a part with PathFinder

  1. On the PathFinder tab in an assembly, highlight the part you want to select by hovering over its name in PathFinder.

  2. Click the part.

To clear a selection

Do either of the following:

  • Click in free space.

  • Select another part.

Tip:

  • To select a list of parts, click the first part in the list, then hold down the Shift key and select the last part in the list.

  • To select more than one non-sequential part, hold down the Ctrl key, and then click the parts you want.

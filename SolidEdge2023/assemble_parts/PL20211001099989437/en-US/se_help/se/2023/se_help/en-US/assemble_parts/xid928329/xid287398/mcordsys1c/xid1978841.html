Edit a Match Coordinate Systems single relationship

  1. In PathFinder, right-click the Match Coordinate Systems relationship and then click Edit Definition, as shown next.

    The part is highlighted.

  2. On the command bar, click the Target Part - Element option.

  3. Click the coordinate system to which you want to match the part.

    The part is aligned to the new coordinate system.

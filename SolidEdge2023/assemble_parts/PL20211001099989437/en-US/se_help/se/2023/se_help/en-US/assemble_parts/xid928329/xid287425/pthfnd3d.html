PathFinder tab (Assembly environment)

Provides alternate ways of viewing an assembly. For a more in-depth explanation of the capabilities of PathFinder, see the PathFinder in assemblies topic.

Note:

When you select a component in PathFinder, you can use the commands on the shortcut menu to show, hide, activate, and inactivate parts and assemblies.

Top Pane

Displays the parts, assemblies, and sketches that make up the assembly.

Bottom Pane

Displays the relationships used to position the selected part or assembly.

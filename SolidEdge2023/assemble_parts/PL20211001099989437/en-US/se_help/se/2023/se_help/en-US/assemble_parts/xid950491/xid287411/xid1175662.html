Isolate parts

  1. Open an assembly document.

  2. Select the parts that you want to only display (isolate).

  3. Right-click, and from the shortcut menu, choose the Isolate command.

  4. In the graphic window, click the Restore button (1) to return to the pre-isolate view or click the Dismiss button (2) to keep the isolated view.

Remove frames or time from an animation event

  1. On the Animation Editor tool, in the right pane, click an event duration bar for an animation event.

  2. Right-click to display the shortcut menu, and then click Remove Frames to display the Remove Frames dialog box.

  3. On the Remove Frames dialog box, specify:

    • The number of frames or the amount of time you want to remove.

    • The position in the animation event from which you want to remove frames or time. For example, you can specify the whether you want to remove the frames or time before or after a particular frame number.

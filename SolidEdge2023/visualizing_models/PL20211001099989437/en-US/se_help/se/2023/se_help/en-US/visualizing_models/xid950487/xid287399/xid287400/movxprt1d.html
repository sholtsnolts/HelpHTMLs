Drag Component command bar

Select Part

Specifies which part you want to move.

Move Selected Part

Moves only the selected part.

Move Dependent Parts

Moves the selected part plus its dependent parts.

Back

Moves the part back one step in the logged steps of movement.

Forward

Moves the part forward one step in the logged steps of movement.

Move

Moves the part parallel to the selected axis.

Rotate

Rotates the part about the selected axis.

Move Planar

Moves the part within a plane you define. The movement plane is defined by the X axis and another axis you select.

Orient Triad From Geometry

Reorients the movement triad of unexploded parts based on selected geometry orientation.

Rotate Triad

Rotates the part movement triad about the x-axis.

Deselect (x)

Clears the selection.

Accept (check mark)

Accepts the selection.

Distance

Displays the linear distance that the part was moved if you move the part by dragging it. Sets the distance to move the part if you type a value.

Angle

Displays the angular distance that the part was rotated if you rotate the part by dragging it. Sets the angular distance to rotate the part if you type a value.

Unbind a subassembly

  1. In the Explode-Render-Animate environment, click the PathFinder tab.

  2. In PathFinder, in the top pane, click a subassembly that was previously grouped with the Bind Subassembly command.

  3. Choose the Home tab→Explode group→Unbind command .

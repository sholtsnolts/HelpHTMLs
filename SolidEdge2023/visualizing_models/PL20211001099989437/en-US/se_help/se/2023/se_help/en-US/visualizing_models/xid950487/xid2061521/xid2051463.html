Visual Explode command bar

Slider

Explodes an assembly dynamically.

  • For dynamic explode, you can move the slider to the right (to explode) or to the left (to collapse).

  • To explode or collapse one step at a time, you can click the slider bar on the right or the left side of the slider respectively.

Explode stepwise

If selected, explodes top-level parts first, then subassemblies, and then node subassemblies and so on.

Close

Discards any input and exits the command.

Note:

When you click Close, the exploded view will be reset (unexploded).

thread axis tolerance

In a drawing view, a function that specifies the angular tolerance between the axis of a hole and the hole's parallelness or perpendicularity to its face (the drawing view normal).  If the axis is within the thread axis tolerance, the thread graphic is displayed on the hole in the drawing view.  If the axis is outside the thread axis tolerance, the thread graphic is not displayed in the drawing view.

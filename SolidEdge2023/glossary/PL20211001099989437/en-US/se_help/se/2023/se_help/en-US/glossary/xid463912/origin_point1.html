origin point

The origin for coordinate readouts displayed by the grid. The origin point is marked by the intersection of the X and Y origin lines.

In synchronous modeling, the grid origin point also is synonymous with the sketch plane origin.

The display colors match the colors of the X and Y axes of the profile sketch or the sketch plane origin triad.

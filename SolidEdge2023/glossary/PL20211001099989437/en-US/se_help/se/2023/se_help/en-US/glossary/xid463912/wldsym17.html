symbol modifier

Controls whether symbol modifiers are displayed.

In Field

Displays the symbol that shows that the weld is to be made in the field.

All Around

Displays the symbol that shows that the weld is to be made all around the weld joint.

Z Modifier

Controls whether the Z modifier symbol is displayed. If the Z modifier is displayed, two additional boxes for text entry are displayed to the right of the Z modifier.

silhouette edge

A theoretical edge that exists when a curved surface (A) is viewed from the side (B).

Although a physical edge does not exist, certain operations in Solid Edge allow you to use this theoretical edge. For example, you can add a connect relationship (C) between a profile line and a silhouette edge to create a cutout feature.

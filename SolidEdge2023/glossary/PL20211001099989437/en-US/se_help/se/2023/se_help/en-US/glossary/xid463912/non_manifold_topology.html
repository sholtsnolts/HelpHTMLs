non-manifold topology

Two or more faces on one body that are connected at a theoretical point or line and form a zero thickness edge.

Non-manifold topology is typically not allowed in Solid Edge when it occurs in a part or sheet metal document. When you construct or edit features that result in non-manifold topology, an error occurs, and the Error Assistant command is activated.

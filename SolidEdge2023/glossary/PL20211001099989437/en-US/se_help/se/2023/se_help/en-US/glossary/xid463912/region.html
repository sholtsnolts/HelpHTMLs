region

A helper object that makes it easier to create surface and solid geometry from sketch elements, or a combination of sketch elements and model edges.

Regions are created by Solid Edge when a series of sketch elements or model edges form a closed area. Regions are displayed using shaded colors, and the color used indicates whether the region consists entirely of sketch elements, or is a combination of sketch elements and edges.

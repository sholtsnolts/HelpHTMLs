Family of Parts Member dialog box

Selects the family of parts member you want to use as the replacement part.

Member

Lists the family of parts member names for the part you are replacing.

Search

Displays the Search dialog box so you can define search criteria to help you locate a specific document.

Browse

Displays the Replacement Part dialog box so you can specify the replacement part. This option is useful when you are replacing a family of parts member with a part that is not in the current part family.

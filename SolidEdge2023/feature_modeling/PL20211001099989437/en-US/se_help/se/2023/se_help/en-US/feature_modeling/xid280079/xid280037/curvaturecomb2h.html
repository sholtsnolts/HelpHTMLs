Display the curvature comb for a 3D curve

  1. Choose Inspect tab→Analyze group→Curvature Comb Settings.

  2. On the Curvature Comb Settings dialog box, click the Show Curvature Curve Settings option.

  3. Click a 3D curve.

Tip:

To clear the display of all curvature combs, you can also click the Clear All Combs option on the Curvature Comb Settings dialog box. To display the Curvature Comb Settings dialog box, choose Inspect tab→Analyze group→Curvature Comb Settings.

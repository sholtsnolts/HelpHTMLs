Draw a circular pattern sketch

Note:

Drawing ordered circular pattern profiles is part of the pattern feature construction process. The Circular Pattern command is available when constructing a pattern feature or when drawing an ordered sketch.

  1. Choose Home tab→Features group→Circular Pattern .

  2. Click where you want the center of the pattern circle or arc to be.

  3. Click where you want the pattern to start.

  4. Click to position the direction arrow on the pattern.

  5. Use the boxes on the Circular Pattern command bar to define other characteristics of the pattern, such as the number of pattern occurrences, radius value, and count.

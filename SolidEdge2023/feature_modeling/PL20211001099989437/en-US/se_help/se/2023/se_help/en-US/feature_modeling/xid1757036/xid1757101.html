Torus command bar

Selection Type

Sets the method for creating a subdivision torus cage.

At Origin

Creates a torus cage at the origin of the Base Coordinate System axially aligned with the Z-axis.

By Axis and 2 Points

Creates a torus cage by defining the center, torus axis, the cage major diameter (including segment angle), and the cage minor diameter (including the segment angle).

Major diameter segments

Specifies the number of segments used for the major diameter of the torus cage.

Minor diameter segments

Specifies the number of segments used for the minor diameter of the torus cage.

Keypoints

Controls keypoint selection on the model.

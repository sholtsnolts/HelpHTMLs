Thin Wall command bar

  • Open Faces

    Defines the open faces for the thin wall feature.

  • Exclude/Include

    Defines the faces you want to include or exclude from the thin wall.

    To exclude a face from the thin wall feature being created, click the button, and then click the face you want to exclude.

    If you want to include a face in an existing thin wall, click the button, and then click the face you want to include.

Toggle Design/Construction Body command

In a multi-body part or sheet metal file, you can switch a solid body type to construction or design. When you switch a design body to a construction body, the construction body appears in the Construction Bodies collector.

Note:

The toggle command only applies to solid bodies. Open volumes (detached bodies) cannot be toggled.

The icon in front of the solid body in PathFinder denotes a construction body type.

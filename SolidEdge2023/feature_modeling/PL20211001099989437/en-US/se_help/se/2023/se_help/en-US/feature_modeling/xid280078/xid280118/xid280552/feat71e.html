Selected set of faces do not form closed offset volume

Thin Region features can fail if the cap faces are not large enough for the extended thin faces to intersect them. The thin faces should be extendable across edges where they connect to the rest of the model to cap a face. Be sure that you select a sufficient number of cap faces.

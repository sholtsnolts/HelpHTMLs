Construct an ordered thin wall solid

  1. Choose Home tab→Solids group→Thin Wall .

  2. On the command bar, type a value for the common wall thickness.

    This is the thickness you want to use for all or most of the part walls.

  3. Click any faces you want to leave open, and then click the Accept button on the command bar.

    Note:

    You can use the Deselect button on the command bar to clear selected faces.

  4. Preview the feature by clicking the Preview button on the command bar.

    Note:

    If the feature is not what you wanted, you can go to any step to make adjustments. You can preview and alter the feature as many times as necessary before going to the finish step.

  5. Finish the feature.

    How To
Tip:

  • Open walls are optional. You can preview the feature any time after you define the common wall thickness.

  • You can use the Thin Wall command bar to change the offset direction from Inside to Outside or Symmetric. Click one of these buttons before you type a common wall thickness, or return to the common wall thickness step later and change the offset.

  • You can make some walls thicker or thinner than the common wall thickness. Click the Unique Thickness button on the command bar to go to the Unique Thickness step. Click the faces you want to apply the unique thickness to and type a unique thickness value in the Unique Thickness box.

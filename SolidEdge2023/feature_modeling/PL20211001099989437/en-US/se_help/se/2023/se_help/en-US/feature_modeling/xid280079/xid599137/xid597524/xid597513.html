Create and display a reflective plane

  1. Do one of the following:

    • Choose Surfacing tab→Inspect group→Reflective Plane Settings .

    • Choose Inspect tab→Analyze group→Reflective Plane Settings .

  2. Click the base planes to display reflections.

  3. To offset a reflective plane, click the Distance field and type an offset value. The value must be greater than zero.

  4. To reverse a reflective plane offset direction, click the Flip button.

  5. To apply a transparency display for active reflective planes, click the Transparency button. Use the slider to control the transparency value or type a value in the Transparency field.

  6. To turn on the display of reflective planes on or off, do one of the following:

    • Choose the Surfacing tab→Inspect group, and select or deselect the Reflective Plane Show button.

    • Choose the Surfacing tab→Inspect group, and select or deselect the Reflective Plane Show button.

Construct a parting surface

  1. Choose Surfacing tab→Surfacing group→Split list→Parting Surface .

  2. Select a planar face or reference plane that is parallel to the direction you want the parting surface.

  3. Select a curve.

  4. Type the surface width you want, then position the cursor in the direction that you want the parting surface and click.

  5. Click Finish.

Tip:

The curve you select can be open or closed. When selecting more than one curve, the curves must be end-point connected.

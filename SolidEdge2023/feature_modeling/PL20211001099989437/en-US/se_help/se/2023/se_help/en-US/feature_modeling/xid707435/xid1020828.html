Recognize a pattern

  1. Select all of the faces of the pattern master.

    These faces are used to detect if they are in a circular or rectangular pattern arrangement in the model.

  2. Choose the Home tab→Pattern group→Pattern list→Recognize Patterns command.

  3. If a pattern is detected, the Pattern Recognition dialog box appears.

    Do one of the following:

    • Accept the patterns found by clicking OK.

    • If you do not want to accept the patterns found, click Cancel or uncheck the pattern recognized and then click OK.

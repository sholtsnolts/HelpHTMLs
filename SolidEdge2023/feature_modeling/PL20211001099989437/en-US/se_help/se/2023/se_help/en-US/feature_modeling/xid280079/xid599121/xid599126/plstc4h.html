Insert a new division into a divided part

  1. Choose Surfacing tab→Surfaces group→Replace Face list→Divide Part .

  2. On the Divide Part dialog box, click an existing division and then click the Insert button.

    How To

  3. Click a reference plane or construction surface to define where you want to divide the part.

  4. Click to define the cutting direction.

  5. On the command bar, click Finish.

  6. On the Divide Part dialog box, in the Filename column, type in the name for the new part document.

  7. Click the new division, then click the Save Selected Files button.

Note:

If any parts need to be updated after the new division is inserted, it will be reflected in the status column. To update a part, select it, then click the Save Selected Files button.

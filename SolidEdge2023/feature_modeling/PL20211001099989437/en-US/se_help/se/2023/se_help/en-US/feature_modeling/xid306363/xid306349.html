Union selected bodies

  1. Open a multi-body file.

  2. Choose Home tab→Solids group→Add Body list→Union.

  3. Select the target body.

  4. Select the tool bodies. You can select design or construction bodies.

  5. On the command bar, click the Accept button.

    Note:

    The target body consumes the tool bodies.

  6. On the command bar, click the Finish button.

  7. Click Cancel or press the Esc key to end the Union command.

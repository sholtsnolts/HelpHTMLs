Edit the location of a decal

  1. In PathFinder, right-click the decal entry, and then click Edit Definition.

  2. Click the dimension and type in a new value.

  3. Press Tab to enter the new value.

    Notice that the new position projects the image above the face, and the image is trimmed by the edge of the face.

  4. On the Decal command bar, click the Face Step button

    .

  5. Click an additional face on which to project the image.

  6. Click Accept .

    The complete image crosses multiple faces.

  7. Click Finish.

Round command

In ordered modeling:

Use the Round command to round the edges of a part. You can use a constant rounding radius, a variable radius, or a combination of the two. You can also create a blend between edges, faces, or a combination of the two.

In synchronous modeling:

Use the Round command to apply a constant radius round to the edges of a part.

Extend a surface

  1. Choose Surfacing tab→Surfaces group→Extend Surface .

  2. Select the edge of the surface that you want to extend, then click the Accept button.

  3. Define the distance you want to extend the surface using your cursor or by typing the distance on the command bar.

  4. Finish the feature.

Chamfer Edge

Specifies how the chamfer geometry is defined.

  • Equal Setbacks—Allows you to define one setback distance, which is applied equally to both adjacent faces.

  • Angle and Setback—Allows you to define a setback distance for one face and the angle of the chamfer. The setback on the other adjacent face is calculated automatically.

  • 2 Setbacks—Allows you to define a setback distance for each adjacent face. The angles are calculated automatically.

Construct a web network

  1. Choose Home tab→Solids group→Thin-Wall list→Web Network .

  2. Select one or more sketch elements.

  3. Do one of the following, depending on your environment:

    • In the synchronous environment, type the rib thickness in the value edit box and press the Tab key. Set the rib extent options. Click the Draft option to apply draft to all faces of the web network.

    • In the ordered environment, on the command bar, type the rib thickness and press the Enter key. Set the rib extent options. Click the Treatment option to apply draft to all faces of the web network.

  4. Click to define the direction you want to project the sketch to form the body of the web network.

  5. Do one of the following, depending on your environment:

    • In the synchronous environment, on the command bar, click the Accept button to finish the feature.

    • In the ordered environment, on the command bar, click the Finish button to finish the feature.

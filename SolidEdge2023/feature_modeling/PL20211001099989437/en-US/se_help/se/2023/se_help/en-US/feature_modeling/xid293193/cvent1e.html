This topic gives you solutions to problems you might have when constructing vents.

Missing parent

The boundary cannot intersect the solid model

The boundary element of the sketch that defines the vent feature cannot pass through any surfaces on the design model.  You can avoid this error by drawing the sketch on a reference plane that is offset from an exterior face on the model.

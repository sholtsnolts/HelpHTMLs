Display zebra stripes on a part

To turn on or turn off zebra stripes:

  • Choose Inspect tab→Analyze group→Zebra Stripes→Show.

  • To define zebra stripes settings:

    1. Choose Inspect tab→Analyze group→ Zebra Stripes Settings.

    2. On the Zebra Stripes Settings dialog box, define the zebra stripes settings you want.

Tip:

  • To view zebra stripes on a model you must also shade the active window using the Shaded or Shaded With Visible Edges commands.

  • You can use the Zebra Stripes dialog box to control the zebra stripe properties, such as color, spacing and mapping method.

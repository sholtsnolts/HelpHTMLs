Edit a bend angle directly

  1. Choose Home tab→Modify group→Resize list→Resize Bend Angle .

  2. Select the bend(s) that you want to modify.

  3. Select the faces that remain stationary.

  4. Type the bend angle you want, and then press the Enter key.

  5. Finish the feature.

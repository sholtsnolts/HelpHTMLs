Hole Circle command

The Hole Circle command is started automatically by the Home tab→Solids group→Hole command (ordered) in ordered part or in assembly.

After you define a profile sketch plane where you want to place the holes, the Hole Circle command bar is displayed for you to place one or more circles to construct a hole feature in your part.

Example:

When you close the sketch profile, the Hole command bar is displayed again for you to finish the Hole feature.

For more information, see Place hole circles and Construct a hole (ordered).

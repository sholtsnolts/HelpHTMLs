Arranging table pages

When a table contains multiple pages, each additional page is automatically joined to the previous page. When you drag any page of the table, all of the pages move together.

You can rearrange individual pages in a multi-page table.

  • You can separate the group of pages into individual pages. Then you can move each table page so that it resides on the same sheet as the drawing view it documents.

  • You can rejoin table pages that have been separated, as long as they reside on the same sheet.

  • You cannot rejoin pages out of sequence. Even after they have been rearranged, table pages retain their ownership and order.

For more information, see Separate and rejoin table pages.

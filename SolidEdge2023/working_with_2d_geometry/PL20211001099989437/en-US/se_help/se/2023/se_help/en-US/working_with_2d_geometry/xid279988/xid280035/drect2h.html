Draw a rectangle using two points

  1. Choose the Rectangle by 2 Points command .

  2. Click where you want one corner of the rectangle to be (A).

  3. Dynamically drag, and then click to define the diagonal corner of the rectangle (B) or enter values in the edit boxes..

Tip:

  • Instead of clicking to draw the rectangle, you can type values in the Width, Height, and Angle boxes on the Rectangle command bar. You can also use a combination of graphic and edit box input.

  • You can use the options on the command bar to edit a rectangle.

  • You can press and hold the Shift key to draw a square.

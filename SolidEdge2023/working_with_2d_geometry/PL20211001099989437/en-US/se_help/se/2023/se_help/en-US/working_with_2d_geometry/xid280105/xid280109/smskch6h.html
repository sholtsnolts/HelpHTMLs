Example: Establish More Than One Relationship

  1. Click the Line command.

  2. Click where you want the line to begin (1).

  3. Move the cursor to recognize the point on element and tangent relationships with a circle on the drawing sheet (2).

  4. While the point on element and tangent IntelliSketch relationship indicators are displayed at the cursor, click to define the end point of the line (3).

    IntelliSketch places a relationship handle for each recognized relationship.

Ungroup command

The Ungroup command removes elements from groups that were defined with the Group command. You can remove individual elements from a group.

How do I
Group or ungroup elements
Hide or show a group of elements
Make a group or groups of elements locatable
Make a group or groups of elements non-locatable
Collapse and expand groups

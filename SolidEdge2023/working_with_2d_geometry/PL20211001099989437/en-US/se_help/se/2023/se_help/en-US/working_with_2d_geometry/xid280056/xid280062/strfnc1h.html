Stretch the contents of a fence

  1. Select the Stretch command .

  2. Click to define the first corner of the fence.

  3. Click to define the second, diagonal corner of the fence.

  4. Click to define the stretch-from point.

  5. Click to define the stretch-to point.

Tip:

  • Elements that are completely inside the fence are moved. Elements that overlap the fence are stretched.

  • Depending on how the stretched elements are modified, some relationships may be removed to complete the stretch operation.

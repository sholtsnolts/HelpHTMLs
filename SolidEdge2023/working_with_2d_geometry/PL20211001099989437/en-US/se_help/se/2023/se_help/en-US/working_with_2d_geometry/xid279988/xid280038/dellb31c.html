Ellipse By 3 Points command

The Ellipse By 3 Points command draws an ellipse using three edge points. The first two points define the length of the primary axis and the rotation angle. The last point defines the secondary axis.

Note:

If the ellipse is aligned horizontally or vertically you can make it adapt variationally to changes in the model by dimensioning its major or minor axis, or constraining a silhouette point on the ellipse to another element.

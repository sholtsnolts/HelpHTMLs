Group command

Binds elements so you can locate, select, and manipulate them as a unit. Grouped elements are usually related, such as the holes and centerlines of a bolt hole pattern. When you select a group, the Group command bar is displayed so you can edit the entire group or any of its members.

Note:

You can view and manage the groups you create using the Groups tab in the Library window.

How do I
Group or ungroup elements
Hide or show a group of elements
Make a group or groups of elements locatable

Change automatic dimension display

To turn automatic dimensioning on and off, do one of the following:

  • To turn on, set the Auto-Dimension command .

  • To turn off, clear the Auto-Dimension command.

Tip:

You also can turn automatic dimensioning on or off by selecting or clearing the Automatically create dimension for new geometry check box in the Auto-Dimension page (IntelliSketch dialog box).

Make elements or key points horizontal or vertical

  1. Choose Home or Sketching tab→Relate group→Horizontal/Vertical .

  2. Do one of the following:

    • To make a line horizontal or vertical, click the line (A).

    • To make two key points horizontal or vertical, click a key point, and then click another key point.

    Tip:

    The line's current orientation determines how it is positioned after you select it. For example, if a line is closer to a horizontal orientation than a vertical orientation, it becomes horizontal.

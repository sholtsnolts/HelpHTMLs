Connect elements

  1. Choose Home or Sketching tab→Relate group→Connect .

  2. Click an element at a key point.

  3. Click another element or key point (A). One element moves to connect the elements (B).

    Tip:

    You can also use the command to create a connect relationship between an element and a series of endpoint connected elements, such that the series acts as a single element. This group can be a combination of lines, arcs, elliptical arcs, and open b-spline curves. The series of elements can be open or closed, but cannot be disjoint. All elements in the series must be endpoint connected.

Geometry page (Relationship Assistant)

Sets which relationships are applied by the Relationship Assistant command. Set the relationship types you want to apply, and clear the relationship types you do not want to apply.

The tolerance fields set the distance and angular tolerances. These tolerances determine how horizontal, vertical, and connecting relationships are interpreted. You may find it helpful to adjust these settings to allow broader relationship interpretation under some circumstances—with imported geometry, for example.

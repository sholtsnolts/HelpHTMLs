Make elements equal

  1. Choose Home tab→Relate group→ Equal .

  2. Click an element.

  3. Click another element (A). The elements become equal (B).

    Tip:

    • You can make the following elements equal: two lines, two arcs, two circles, and an arc and a circle.

    • You cannot make a line equal to an arc or circle.

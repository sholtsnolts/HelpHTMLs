Example: Draw an arc

  1. Click the Arc By Center Point command.

  2. Click where you want to place the center point (1).

  3. Move the cursor to where you want the arc to start and click (2).

  4. Move the cursor to where you want the arc to end and click (3).

    You can type values in the radius and angle fields.

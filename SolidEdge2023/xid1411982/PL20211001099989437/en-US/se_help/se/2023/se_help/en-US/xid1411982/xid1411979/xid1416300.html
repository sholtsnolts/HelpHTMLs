Open a work item in Polarion

With a Solid Edge document open, you can view detailed information about requirements by opening the associated work item in Polarion.

  1. Open a Solid Edge document that includes requirement work item IDs.

  2. In the Requirements docking pane, select one or more work item IDs and click Open .

  3. Log into Polarion if prompted.

    The work item details are displayed in Polarion.

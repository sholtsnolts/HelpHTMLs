Publish a Solid Edge document to a work item

After you make changes to a Solid Edge document, you may want to attach a new 3D PDF of the document to the selected work item in Polarion.

  1. In the Requirements docking pane, select the work item ID.

  2. Click Publish .

    A comment and a 3D PDF of the active Solid Edge document is added to the work item in Polarion. When you publish a draft document, a 2D PDF is attached. The PDF can be opened from the Attachments portion of the Polarion work item. Polarion records each revision of the published PDF document.

Note:

If you open the PDF from Polarion and no 3D content is displayed, ensure the display of 3D content is enabled for your viewer.

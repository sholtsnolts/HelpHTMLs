Center of Mass and Coordinate System points

When physical properties are defined in the model and shown in the drawing view, the point that represents the center of mass and the location of the coordinate system is selected for placing coordinate dimensions.

Note:

For more information, see Display a coordinate system or center of mass in a drawing view.

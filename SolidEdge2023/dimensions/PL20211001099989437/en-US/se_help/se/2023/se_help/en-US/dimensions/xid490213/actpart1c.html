Activate Part command

Activates a part in a draft quality view. When you activate a part, its model data is loaded into memory so that it can be dimensioned, used in relationships with other elements, and so forth. Because draft quality views do not display hidden edges, hidden edges remain unavailable for dimensioning even after the part is activated. The draft quality view must be up-to-date before you can activate a part in it.

Remove breaks from a dimension projection line

  1. Right-click a dimension from which you want to remove projection line breaks.

  2. On the dimension shortcut menu, choose Remove Projection Line Break.

Tip:

You also can use Edit→Undo and Edit→Redo for breaks that you have just removed.

Add a dimension to a dimension group

  1. Click one of the following buttons:

    • Distance Between

    • Angle Between

    • Symmetric Diameter

    • Coordinate Dimension

  2. Click a dimension (A) in a dimension group.

  3. Click an element (B) you want to measure.

  4. Click where you want the dimension (C). This adds the dimension to the dimension group.

Tip:

  • If you delete a dimension from the middle of a chained dimension group, the group is split into two groups.

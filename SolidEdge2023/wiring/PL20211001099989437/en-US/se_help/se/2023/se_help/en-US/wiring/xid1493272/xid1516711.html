Update the harness design to match Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design

  • In Electrical Routing, click Home→Electrical→Update Harness .

Note:

The system deletes any elements that exist in the harness, but not in Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design, and adds any elements that exist in Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design, but not in the harness.

For more information, see Working with Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design.

Create a connector table

  1. Choose Diagram tab→Tables group→Connector Table .

  2. Click a connector drawing view.

  3. To show connector callouts, on the Connector Table command bar, click Auto-Callout.

  4. Click the Properties button to edit the table properties or click to place the table.

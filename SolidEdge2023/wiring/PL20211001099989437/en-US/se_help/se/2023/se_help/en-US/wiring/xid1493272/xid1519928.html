Cross select objects between Solid Edge and Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design

  1. In Electrical Routing, click Home→Electrical→Cross Select .

  2. In Solid Edge or Solid Edge Wiring and Harness Design select an object(s).

    The object is cross selected in the other application.

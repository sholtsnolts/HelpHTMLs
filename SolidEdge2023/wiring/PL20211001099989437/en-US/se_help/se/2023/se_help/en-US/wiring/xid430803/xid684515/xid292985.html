Insert Bend command

Inserts a bend on the flattened geometry in the nailboard drawing view. Solid Edge inserts the bend by editing the radius and sweep angle, while maintaining the overall length of the branch.

You can use the Flip option on the Insert Bend command bar to flip the direction of the bend.

Record Video dialog box

Recording

The options in the Recording section are all that you need to record a video.

Record (Shift+F9)

Starts recording the video and displays the Pause and Stop controls.

Pause/Start (Shift+F9)

Pauses the video. You can resume or stop the video.

Stop/Finish (Shift+F10)

Stops and finalizes the recording currently in progress. The recorded video automatically plays in the Windows default media player.

Play

Opens the video that was just recorded in the Windows default media player.

Duration

Displays the elapsed time as the video is recording.

File Size

Displays the file size as the video is recording.

Hide this dialog while recording

Hides the dialog during recording. Click the Record button at the bottom of the application window to show controls during recording.

Area to record

This option defines the area to record.

Drop list (Active Document Window is the default)

Drop list for area to record includes Custom Area, Solid Edge Application, and Active Document Window.

Define Area

When you select Custom Area as area to record, the Define Area button activates. Click the Define Area button and a cross-hair appears. Click and drag to define the area to record. You can define an area anywhere on any screen (inside or outside the Solid Edge window).

Audio

Records accompanying audio with video in Solid Edge.

Record audio

Turn on or off the audio during a recording.

Audio input device

Choose the audio input device to be used for recording.

Options

Displays the Compression Codec drop list

Audio settings

Launches the Windows sound control panel where the recording level can be adjusted.

Discard

Discards the video you just recorded and returns all settings to default.

Save

Saves the last recording that was made.

Upload

Launches the Upload video to YouTube dialog box.

Help

Displays the Help topic for YouTube videos in Solid Edge.

Record a video

  1. On the status bar in the bottom-right corner of the Application window, choose the Record command .

  2. On the Record Video dialog box, define the area to record.

  3. Click the Record button.

  4. To Pause, click the Pause button.

    The Pause button changes to a Record button to resume recording.

  5. To end the recording, press the Stop button.

    The recorded video automatically plays in the default Windows media player.

  6. Close the player

  7. On the Record Video dialog box you can do the following:

    • Click Discard to delete the video you just recorded and return all settings to default.

    • Click Save to save the recording.

    • Click Upload to launch the Upload to YouTube dialog box.

  8. Close the Record Video dialog box.

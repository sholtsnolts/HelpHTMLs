Web Publisher Wizard (Publish Format)

Web pages may be delivered as a group of files or as a single self-extracting archive file in ZIP format. If the files are bundled in an archive file, they may be distributed via e-mail directly from IPA.

Wizard Options
E-mail published pages

If checked, the wizard will produce an self-extracting archive file and attach it to a mail message with the information entered on the e-mail page which follows.

Save pages to self-extracting executable

If selected, the wizard will produce an self-extracting archive file in the location chosen on a succeeding page.

Save pages to a location

Check this box to save pages as a group of files in a location specified on the upcoming save page.

Note:

The boxes that are checked here determine the pages that are displayed when the Next button is pressed. The pages that follow will vary depending on the choices made on this page.

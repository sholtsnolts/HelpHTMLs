YouTube videos in Solid Edge

Users can directly capture videos inside Solid Edge. You can save these videos locally and also upload them to . Solid Edge has a YouTube tool pane for viewing and searching for Solid Edge related videos. This tool gives the Solid Edge community a mechanism for sharing knowledge and learning from other users.

Tools

  • Record command

  • Upload to YouTube command

  • View and search YouTube tool pane, which you can open by selecting the View tab→Panes group→YouTube command on the ribbon or on the status bar at bottom-right in the application window:

Requirements

  • A Google account is required to upload videos to YouTube.

  • To upload videos to YouTube, the YouTube Upload Solid Edge Administrator option must be enabled. The option is enabled by default. The system administrator can disable the option.

  • To perform YouTube searches and playbacks, the YouTube Search and Playback Solid Edge Administrator option must be enabled. The option is enabled by default. The system administrator can disable the option.

Note:

If the YouTube Upload, Search, and Playback options are disabled, you can remove the YouTube tool pane display by clicking the X on the docking window.

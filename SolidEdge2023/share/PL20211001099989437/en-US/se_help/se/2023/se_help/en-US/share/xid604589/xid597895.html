Upload to YouTube command

Use the Upload to YouTube command to upload a prerecorded video to the YouTube website.

When you select the command, use the Upload to YouTube dialog box to do the following:

  • Browse for a prerecorded video.

  • Define a title and description to the video.

  • Assign classification tags to the video, so that you can find it later.

  • Specify who is authorized to view the video once it is uploaded to YouTube.

  • Upload the video.

Update a drawing view after editing a part or assembly

  1. Double-click a drawing view that is a part view.

    The part, sheet metal, or assembly document associated with the drawing view is opened.

  2. Edit the part, sheet metal, or assembly document.

  3. Return to the draft document. The affected drawing views are displayed with a border indicating that they are out of date. Do any of the following:

    • To update a single drawing view, right-click the drawing view and click Update.

    • To update all the drawing views, choose Home tab→Drawing Views group→Update Views command.

Tip:

Force Drawing Views to Update—If you hold Ctrl+Shift when you click Update Views, Solid Edge performs a full update, just as it would on initial view creation.  That is, it rereads all of the model data to regenerate the view, rather than only the model data thought to have changed.

Convert to High Quality View command

Converts a draft quality view to a high quality view. You can convert all draft quality views in the drawing into high quality views, or you can convert only the selected draft quality view.  Once a draft quality view is converted to a high quality view, you cannot convert it back to a draft quality view.

After you run the command, the selected views become out-of-date, and are converted on the next drawing view update. This command is not available for detail views.

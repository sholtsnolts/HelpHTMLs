Select a drawing sheet

  • Click the tab of the drawing sheet you want to activate.

Tip:

  • You can delete or rename a drawing sheet using the shortcut menu; right-click while the cursor is over a sheet tab.

  • You can double-click a drawing sheet tab to access the Sheet Setup dialog box, or you can use the shortcut menu. To display the shortcut menu, click the right mouse button while the cursor is over a sheet tab.

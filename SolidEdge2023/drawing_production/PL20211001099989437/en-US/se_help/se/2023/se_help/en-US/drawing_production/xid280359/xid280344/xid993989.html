Scale a section view

To change the scale of a 2D section view, you must remove its alignment to the source view.

  1. Right-click a section drawing view, and deselect Maintain Alignment.

  2. Now click the section drawing view, and then on the Drawing View Selection command bar, do either of the following:

    • Choose a scale from the Scale list.

    • Type a custom scale in the Scale value box.

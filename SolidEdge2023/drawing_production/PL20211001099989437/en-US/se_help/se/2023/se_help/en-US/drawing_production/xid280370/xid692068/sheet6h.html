Rename a drawing sheet

  1. On the drawing sheet, click the tab of the sheet you want to rename.

  2. On the File menu, click Settings→Sheet Setup.

  3. In the Sheet Setup dialog box, click the Name tab and type a new name for the sheet you selected.

Tip:

You can also rename a drawing sheet with the Rename command on the shortcut menu when the cursor is over a drawing sheet tab.

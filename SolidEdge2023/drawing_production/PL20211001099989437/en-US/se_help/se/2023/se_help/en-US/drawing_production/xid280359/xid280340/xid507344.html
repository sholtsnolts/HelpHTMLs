Remove Break Line Inheritance command

The Remove Break Line Inheritance shortcut command removes the associative relationship created by the Inherit Break Lines command between the break lines in two drawing views.

Once the associativity is removed, you can select and delete the break lines in the folded view.

Note:

You also can remove the associative relationship using the Delete Alignment shortcut command, or by deselecting the Maintain Alignment command.

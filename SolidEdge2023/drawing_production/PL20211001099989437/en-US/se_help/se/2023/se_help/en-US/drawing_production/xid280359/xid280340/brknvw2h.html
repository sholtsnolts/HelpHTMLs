Display break lines and cropped edges in a broken view

Show break lines in a broken view

To display the break lines in a broken view:

  1. Select the broken drawing view.

  2. On the Drawing View Selection command bar, click the Show Broken View button .

    When this option is cleared, the drawing view is shown in its unbroken state. This provides access to the break lines for editing.

Show cropped edges in a broken view

To hide the break lines and show the cropped edges in the broken view:

  1. Right-click the drawing view and choose Properties.

  2. In the Drawing View Properties dialog box:

    1. On the General page, select the Hide break lines in broken state check box.

    2. On the Annotation page, select the Show boundary edges check box.

  3. Right-click the drawing view and choose Update.

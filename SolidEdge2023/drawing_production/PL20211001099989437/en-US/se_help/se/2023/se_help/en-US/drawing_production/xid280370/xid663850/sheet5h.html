Display background sheets

Do one of the following to display the background sheet:

  • Choose View tab→Sheet Views→Background.

  • Right-click a sheet tab at the bottom of the document and choose Background.

A sheet tab is displayed in the sheet tab tray for each background sheet in the document.

Tip:

  • Each background sheet tab is labeled with the sheet size, such as A4-Sheet and A3-Sheet.

  • The word BACKGROUND appears as a non-printing watermark stamped on each background sheet.

  • You can change the default color of the background sheet tabs on the Colors tab (Solid Edge Options dialog box).

Automatic Centerlines command bar

Dimension Style Mapping

Specifies that the setting on the Dimension Style tab of the Options dialog box determines the dimension style.  When you set this option, Dimension Style is unavailable.

Dimension Style

Lists and applies the available dimension styles.  This control is unavailable when Dimension Style Mapping is enabled.

Center Line and Center Mark Options

Displays the Center Line and Center Mark Options dialog box.

Center Lines

Specifies whether centerlines are added to or removed from the drawing view.

Center Marks

Specifies whether center marks are added to or removed from the drawing view.

Center Mark Projection Lines

Specifies whether center mark projection lines are added to center marks.

Connect Center Marks

Specifies whether center marks are connected with lines when they lie on the same x-axis or y-axis.

Add Lines and Marks

Specifies that you want to add centerlines and marks to the drawing view (according to the command bar settings you specify).

Remove Lines and Marks

Specifies that you want to remove automatically-created centerlines and marks from the drawing view (according to the command bar settings you specify).

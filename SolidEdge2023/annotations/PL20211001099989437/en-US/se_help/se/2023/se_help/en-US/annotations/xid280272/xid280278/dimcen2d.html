Center Mark command bar

Dimension Style Mapping

Specifies that the setting on the Dimension Style tab of the Options dialog box determines the dimension style. When you set this option, Dimension Style is unavailable.

Dimension Style

Lists and applies the available dimension styles. This control is unavailable when Dimension Style Mapping is enabled.

Properties

Displays the Center Line & Center Mark Properties dialog box.

Dimension Axis

Specifies a line to use as the dimension axis. Use this option to place a center mark at an angle.

This button is not available unless the Use Dimension Axis option is selected from the Orientation list.

Orientation

Specifies the orientation of the center mark. You can set the orientation of a center mark to Horizontal/Vertical, to be aligned with the active dimension axis, or to be defined by two points.

Keypoint Attachment

Enables selection of all element keypoints and point-on relationships, including midpoints and endpoints of arcs and lines.  When this option is deselected, you can select only the center points of arcs and circles.

Projection Lines

Displays the projection lines on a center mark. Projection lines are drawn when you have selected a curved element, such as a circle or arc. If you place a center mark in free space, projection lines are not drawn.

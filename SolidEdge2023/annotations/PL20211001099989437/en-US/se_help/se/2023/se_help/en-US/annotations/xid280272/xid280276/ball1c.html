Balloon command

Places an individual balloon annotation. You can use text in the balloons to refer to an element or a point in free space. By modifying the individual balloon properties, you can control the size and shape of the balloon, text presentation, and leader display. You also can control the orientation of stacked balloons.

To automatically add balloons to a drawing view, or to add a ballooned parts list, use the Parts List command or the Family of Assemblies Parts List command .

Place a center axis on a cylinder or hole feature

You can place a PMI center axis on 3D hole features or cylindrical features.

  1. Choose the PMI tab→Annotation group→Centerline command .

  2. On the command bar, click Center Axis .

  3. Select a circular edge or surface on a hole, conical feature, or a cylindrical feature to create the center axis.

    You can also select revolved, extruded, torus, concentric, and swept surfaces.

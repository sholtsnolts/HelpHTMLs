Copy To PMI command

Copies selected 2D annotations and dimensions from a profile-based feature or from a sketch to the PMI model. The reference plane of the sketch element is used to place the annotation or dimension in the 3D model, instead of the active dimension plane.

When 2D annotations and dimensions are copied to the 3D model, they become PMI elements. They are associative to the parent geometry they were copied from.

Note:

  • This command only copies 2D annotations and dimensions that exist in the active document.

  • PMI dimensions are always driven dimensions.

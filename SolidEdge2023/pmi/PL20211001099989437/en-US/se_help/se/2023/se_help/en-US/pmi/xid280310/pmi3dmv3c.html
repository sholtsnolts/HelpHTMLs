Activate or deactivate a view

You can activate the orientation of the selected 3D model view. When you activate a view, all the PMI elements and section views associated with it are also displayed. After activating a model view if you create any PMI information, it is added to that activated model view.

You can deactivate the activated view. To do so, right-click the node of the activated view, and select Deactivate View.

You cannot undo this command.

Enable or disable sketch consumption and dimension migration

To

Do this in PathFinder

Enable sketch consumption.

Right-click a sketch with disabled consumption and click Migrate Geometry and Dimensions.

Disable sketch consumption.

Right-click a sketch with enabled consumption and click Migrate Geometry and Dimensions.
Tip:

  • When this option is enabled for a sketch, the sketch elements are moved to the Used Sketches collection in PathFinder when you use the sketch elements to construct a feature.

  • When this option is disabled for a sketch, the sketch elements are not moved to the Used Sketches collection in PathFinder when you use the sketch elements to construct a feature.

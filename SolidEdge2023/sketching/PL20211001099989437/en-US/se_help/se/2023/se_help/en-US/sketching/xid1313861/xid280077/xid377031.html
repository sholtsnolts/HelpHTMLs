Sort blocks, layers, or groups

Sort blocks or block views

  1. Click the Library tab.

  2. On the Library tab, right-click a block or block view and choose the sorting method.

Sort layers

  1. Click the Layers tab.

  2. On the Layers tab, right-click the Layers collection node and choose a sorting method.

Sort groups

  1. Click the Groups tab.

  2. On the Groups tab, right-click a group and choose a sorting method.

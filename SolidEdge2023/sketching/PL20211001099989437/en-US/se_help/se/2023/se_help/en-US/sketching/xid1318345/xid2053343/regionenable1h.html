Activate or deactivate sketch regions for a sketch

To

Do this in PathFinder

Activate sketch regions for a sketch.

Right-click a sketch with disabled regions, then click Activate Regions.

Deactivate sketch regions for a sketch.

Right-click a sketch with enabled regions, then click Activate Regions.
Tip:

  • When sketch regions are activated for a sketch, the sketch is displayed as a shaded element.

  • When sketch regions are deactivate for a sketch, the sketch is displayed as wireframe elements.

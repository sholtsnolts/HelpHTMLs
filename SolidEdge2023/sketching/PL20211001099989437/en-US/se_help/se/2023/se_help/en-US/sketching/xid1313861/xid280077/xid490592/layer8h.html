Display or hide a layer

  1. Click the Layers tab to list all layers in the document.

  2. On the Layers tab, click the name of the layer you want to show or hide.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • To display the contents of a hidden layer, on the shortcut menu, click the Show command.

    • To hide a displayed layer, on the shortcut menu, click the Hide command.

Show Annotations command

The Show Annotations command is located on the PathFinder Sketch shortcut menu. This setting turns on or off the display of annotations, such as callouts and block labels, contained in the sketch. In the ordered environment, the annotations display while in sketch mode.

Note:

If Accelerated Sketch Display is on, annotations do not display, even if Show Annotations is on.

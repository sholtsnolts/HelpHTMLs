Enable Regions command (3D sketching)

The Enable Regions command is only available in synchronous modeling. When Enable Regions is selected, pausing over a region highlights the region. Clicking the highlighted region starts the extruded feature command. The Enable Regions command is located on the shortcut menu when you right-click a 3D sketch in PathFinder.

Make a sketch combinable or non-combinable

To make a synchronous sketch combinable

In PathFinder, right-click a sketch, then set the Merge with Coplanar Sketches option on the shortcut menu.

To make a synchronous sketch non-combinable

In PathFinder, right-click a sketch, then clear the Merge with Coplanar Sketches option on the shortcut menu.

Tip:

By default, new synchronous sketches you draw are combinable.

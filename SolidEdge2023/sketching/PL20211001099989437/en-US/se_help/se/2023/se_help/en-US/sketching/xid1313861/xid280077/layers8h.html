Delete one or more layers

  1. Click the Layers tab .

  2. Do one of the following:

    • To delete a single layer, right-click the layer name and choose Delete.

    • To delete all unused layers in the document, click the Delete Empty Layers button .

      Tip:

      You also can right-click the Layers collection node and choose Delete Empty Layers.

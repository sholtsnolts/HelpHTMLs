Regions demonstration

Single region

  • A region forms by creating a closed sketch.

Nested regions

  • A region is created inside another region (nested region). Selecting the outermost region treats the internal region as a void or hole. Selecting both the outermost region and the internal region fills in the void.

Region on a face

  • Regions are created on the face of a part body by drawing a sketch on the face.

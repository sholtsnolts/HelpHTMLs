Arc Segment command

Note:

To access XpresRoute, click Tools→Environs→XpresRoute.

Creates a 3D arc path segment. You do not have to pre-define a reference plane on which to place the arc segment. You can derive the arc's plane from the existing segment geometry.

Tip:

You can connect the arc segment to line segments or other arc segments.

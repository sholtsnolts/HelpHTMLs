Apply an axis dimension relationship

  1. Choose Home tab→Relate group→Axis Dimension .

  2. Click on the first point of the dimension.

  3. Click on the second point of the dimension.

  4. On OrientXpres, click an axis to use for the relationship.

Tip:

  • You can create an axis dimension between a tube segment and another path segment, principal plane, or part edge.

  • If you cannot locate elements on one of the parts in the assembly, make sure the part is active. To activate the part, click the Activate button on the command bar part, and then click the part.

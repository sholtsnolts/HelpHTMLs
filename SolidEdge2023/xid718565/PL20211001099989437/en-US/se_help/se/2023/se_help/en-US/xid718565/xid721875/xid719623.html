Bevel Gear Calculated Results tab

The Calculated Results tab provides the options you use to view results of calculation. This tab has 3 areas:

General Results

Displays the input provided for calculation and a few general results for the bevel gear.

Strength Validation

Displays the overall performance of the gear in terms of Pass/Fail. You can also review the allowable stresses of the gear displayed in this area.

Forces

Displays the results related to load carrying capacity of the gear.

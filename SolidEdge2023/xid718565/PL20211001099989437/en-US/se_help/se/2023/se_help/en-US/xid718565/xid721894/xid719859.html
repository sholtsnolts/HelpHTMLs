Worm Gear Material Selection

The Material button is given on Design Parameters tab to select the material of the Worm and Worm Gear.

Click on this button, the Material Selection window will open.

You have to select the material of the Worm and the Worm Gear.

Simply select the material from respective material selection control. The respective strength values will be automatically displayed as per the material, which are stored in database.

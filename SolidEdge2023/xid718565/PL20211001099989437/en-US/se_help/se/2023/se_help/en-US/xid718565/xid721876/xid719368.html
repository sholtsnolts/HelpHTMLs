Standards used for calculating Cams

There is no specific standard available for designing cams. The following book provides information for designing and calculating cams.

Book Name

Author

Publication

Cams for Industry. A Handbook for designers of special purpose machinery.

John Reeve

Mechanical Engineering Publication Limited. London. First published on 1995.

This book contains basic principles, laws and equations for the design and application of cams and cam operated mechanisms.

Standards used for calculating columns

There is no specific standard available for designing shafts, beams or columns. But lot of reference books are available which gives the methods to calculate the various parameters like force, moments, deflection etc. under different loading conditions. We have referred the following Books from reputed Authors and implemented the methodology adapted.

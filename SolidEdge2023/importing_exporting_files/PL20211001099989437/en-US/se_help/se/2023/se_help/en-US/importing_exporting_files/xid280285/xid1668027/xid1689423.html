The positions of all parts in the assembly are maintained in the migrated Solid Edge files.

Also assembly configurations in Creo Elements Direct are migrated to Solid Edge. For each configuration in Creo Elements Direct, a Solid Edge family of assemblies member will be created. The position and show/hide states of parts in the Creo Elements Direct assembly configuration are maintained in the corresponding family of assemblies.

Constraint migration is not supported in Creo Elements Direct data migration.

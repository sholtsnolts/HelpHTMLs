Working with Parasolid mesh data in Solid Edge 3D translation

It is important to understand how Solid Edge handles Parasolid mesh data during 3D translation:

  • During the import of tessellated data from JT, IFC, STL or STEP, a mesh object is created and you can perform boolean operations on the imported mesh object.

  • You can import tessellated data into the synchronous environment.

  • You can export mesh bodies to JT, IFC, 3MF, or STL.

  • Solid Edge does not support the export of mesh bodies to XT based formats, such as SAT, IGES, CATIA V4 and CATIA V5.

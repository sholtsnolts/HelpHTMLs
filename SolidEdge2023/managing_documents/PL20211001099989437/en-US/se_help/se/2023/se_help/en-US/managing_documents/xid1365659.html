Replace Part command bar

The Replace Part command bar options are used to replace a single part or all parts in an assembly with their latest revision or with their latest released revision.

Occurrence Selection

Selects all occurrences of the target part at that level only.

Scroll To

Scrolls through PathFinder to the part you selected.

Zoom To

Fits the view to the selected part.

Ghosted

Shows or hides all parts except those that have newer revisions available.

Select All

Selects all parts that have newer revisions available.

Released Only

Toggles between parts that have newer revisions and newer released revisions available.

Accept (check mark)

Accepts the selection and runs the Replace Part command.

Deselect (X)

Clears the selection.

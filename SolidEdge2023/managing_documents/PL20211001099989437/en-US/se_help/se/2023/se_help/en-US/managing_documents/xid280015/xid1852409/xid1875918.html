Comparing models in Concept Workspace

You can compare your last saved modified document in Concept Workspace to its equivalent in Teamcenter.

  1. While working in Concept Workspace, on the Teamcenter ribbon, click Concept Assistant.

  2. Select a single modified part or sheet metal document you want to compare with its equivalent in Teamcenter.

  3. Click Compare Models .

  4. Compare the server version (top pane) of your part or sheet metal model to the conceptual model (bottom pane).

Edit properties

You can modify some of the properties, such as Doc Number and Project Name, as well as enter comments, in the Comments property.

  1. In the Design Manager document list, click the cell corresponding to the property you want to change and start typing. For example, if you want to add comments to part 123, locate part 123 in the document tree, click the corresponding Comments cell, and enter your comment.

    Design Manager changes the corresponding Action column to display the text, Update Properties.

  2. Do one of the following:

    • To cancel the change, click the Current Filename cell for the document of interest, and choose Home tab→Action group→Clear Action .

      Note:

      To cancel all changes, press Ctrl+A and then click Clear Action.

    • To make the change, choose Home tab→Action group→Perform Actions .

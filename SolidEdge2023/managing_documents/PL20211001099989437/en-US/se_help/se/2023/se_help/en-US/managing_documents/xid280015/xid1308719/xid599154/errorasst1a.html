Error Assistant

The Error Assistant located in the lower-right pane of the Add to Teamcenter Interactive window, provides details and suggestions for corrective action to resolve problems found during your session.

Selecting a cell in the Issues column in either the unmanaged or managed pane of the Add to Teamcenter Interactive window displays information and associated corrective action for the selected issue. The information continues to display until you click a different cell in the Issues column.

Use Active Workspace in Solid Edge

Active Workspace, an interface to Teamcenter, is a personalized dashboard-type tool for accessing product lifecycle management (PLM) information. You can use Active Workspace capabilities in change management, workflow, requirements management, and visualization to help you accomplish your tasks.

There are two implementations of Active Workspace available in Solid Edge:

  • Hosted

    After you define your Teamcenter environment to use an Active Workspace enabled server, access hosted Active Workspace from the quick access toolbar in Solid Edge , or from the File menu→Manage page.

  • Standalone

    Accessed through a browser window where you log into Active Workspace, find Solid Edge data that is managed by Teamcenter, and send the document directly to Solid Edge.

Note:

Both implementations require at least Internet Explorer 11.

Technical support

The Global Customer Support & Success (GCS&S) team provides technical support for Solid Edge customers.

Accessing support from Add to Teamcenter Interactive

You can access many support functions directly from Add to Teamcenter Interactive. On the Help menu, click the appropriate option to access the Support Center website, request a WebKey account, and then explore the online support library.

Contacting support

You can also access GCS&S through Support Center at https://support.sw.siemens.com.

.

Start Design Manager

You can start Design Manager:

  • Independent of Solid Edge.

    • Choose Start→Siemens Solid Edge 2023→Design Manager.

    • In Windows Explorer, right-click a Solid Edge file, and then choose Open with Design Manager.

    Note:

    Although you can use Design Manager outside of Solid Edge, you must still have Solid Edge installed on your machine.

  • From Solid Edge.

    • Choose Data Management tab→Design Manager .

    Note:

    If you launched Design Manager from Solid Edge, you can return to Solid Edge by choosing Home tab→Open In group→Return to Solid Edge.

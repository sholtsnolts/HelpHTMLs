Report Revision Mismatches command

Displays a list of objects in the Error Assistant pane that have been identified as experiencing errors when the draft and linked 3D content’s revision does not match. Errors can occur when a draft is saved to a different revision as the model, and with Family of Parts and Divide Parts that are at different revision levels.

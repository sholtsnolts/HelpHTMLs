Typical Design Manager workflow

This workflow illustrates how you can manage your documents to update a component in one of your products.

  1. Open the part or assembly of interest.

  2. Find all the drawings associated with the documents of interest by performing a where-used search.

  3. Move the part or assembly to a new location.

  4. Revise the part or assembly.

  5. Perform any other actions, as needed.

  6. Apply the changes.

  7. Release the part or assembly to protect it from unauthorized changes.

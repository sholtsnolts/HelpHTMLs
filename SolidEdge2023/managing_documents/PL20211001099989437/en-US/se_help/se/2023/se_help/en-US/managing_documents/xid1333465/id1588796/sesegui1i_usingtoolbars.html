Using the Structure Editor toolbar

Toolbars give you quick access to commands. The Structure Editor toolbar is located at the top of the application window. The toolbar contains commands for printing documents, saving documents, and revising documents. The toolbar also contains list boxes for Show View and Revision Rule.

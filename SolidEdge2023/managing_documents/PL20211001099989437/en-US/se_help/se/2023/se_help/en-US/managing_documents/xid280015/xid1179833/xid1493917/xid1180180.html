Working with standalone Active Workspace

Use your browser to access Active Workspace. Select an Item, Item Revision, or Solid Edge dataset, and use the Open in Solid Edge command to have the associated design data open in Solid Edge. Then you can use the additional capabilities such as:

  • Drag and drop a component into a Solid Edge document.

  • Copy and paste a component into a Solid Edge document.

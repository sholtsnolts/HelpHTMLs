Technical support

The Global Customer Support & Success (GCS&S) team provides technical support for Solid Edge customers.

Accessing support from Solid Edge

You can access many support functions directly from Structure Editor. On the Solid Edge Help pane, click the appropriate option to access the Support Center website.

Contacting support

You can also access GCS&S through Support Center at https://support.sw.siemens.com.

For problems relating to Microsoft, contact Microsoft support on the Web:

http://support.microsoft.com

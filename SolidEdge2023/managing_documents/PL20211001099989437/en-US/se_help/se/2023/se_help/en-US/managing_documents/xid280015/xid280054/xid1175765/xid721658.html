Checking documents in and out

When you work in a Teamcenter-managed environment, you can check documents in and out of Teamcenter using the Manage shortcut commands in PathFinder.

This option:

Enables you to:

Check Out

Check out the selected document from the library.

Check Out All

Check out the selected document and its children from the library.

Cancel Check Out

Cancels the check out of the selected document.

Cancel Check Out All

Cancels the check out of the selected document and its children.

Check In

Check the selected document into the library.

Check In All

Check the selected document and its children into the library.

Note:

Unrelated parts and drawings are not checked in.

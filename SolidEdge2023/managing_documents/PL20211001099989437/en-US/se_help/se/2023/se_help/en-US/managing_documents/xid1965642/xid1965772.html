Add to Teamcenter-Interactive video

Add to Teamcenter-Interactive provides you an interactive process for adding Solid Edge 3D and draft data into Teamcenter. With a focus on working with unmanaged data that is of a product or project in size, it is particularly suited for supply chain collaboration and reintegration of formerly managed content into Teamcenter.

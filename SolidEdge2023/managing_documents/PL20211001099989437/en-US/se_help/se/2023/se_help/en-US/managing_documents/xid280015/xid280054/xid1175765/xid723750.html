Check documents in and out

From PathFinder

  1. In PathFinder, right-click the document you want to check in or check out.

  2. Click Manage, and then select Check In, Check Out, or Cancel Checkout.

From Teamcenter tab

  1. With a Teamcenter-managed document open in Solid Edge, select the object you want to check in or check out.

  2. Select Teamcenter tab→Manage group, and then select Check In, Check Out, or Cancel Checkout.

Note:

To check in all documents, not just the selected document and its children, use the Check In All command available in the Cache Assistant.

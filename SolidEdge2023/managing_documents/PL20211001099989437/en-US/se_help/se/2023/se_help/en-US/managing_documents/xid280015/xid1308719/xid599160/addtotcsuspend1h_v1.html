Suspend or pause Add to Teamcenter

  1. While Add to Teamcenter is running, click Suspend on the Add to Teamcenter Status dialog box.

    Your request to suspend the process is pending until the current block of files completes the Add to Teamcenter process.

  2. Respond to the prompt to either Suspend or Pause the process.

    When you choose to Suspend the process, the next time you run Add to Teamcenter, you are given the option to restart the remaining actions and continue processing the next block of files.

    When you choose to Pause the process, you can continue with the next block of files by clicking Resume.

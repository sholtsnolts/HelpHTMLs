Delete documents from the local cache

  1. On the Cache Assistant dialog box, select the document(s) you want to delete from cache.

  2. Click the right mouse button to display the shortcut menu.

  3. On the shortcut menu, click Delete From Cache.

  4. On the Confirm Delete All dialog box, click Yes.

Tip:

You can click Delete All on the Cache Assistant dialog box to delete all documents and folders from the local cache.

Revisions command

Creates or displays revisions of the direct document when no document is selected, or all the documents that are within a selection set. A where-used search is performed to find associated draft documents. The draft documents are revised and checked in or uploaded along with the active document as determined by the setting of the Automatically revise or copy drawings that use the selected 3D document check box. The check box is on the Manage page of the Solid Edge Options dialog box. Any parts or subassemblies which may have been added to the target subassembly out of context are downloaded to cache to ensure the structure and properties are up-to-date.

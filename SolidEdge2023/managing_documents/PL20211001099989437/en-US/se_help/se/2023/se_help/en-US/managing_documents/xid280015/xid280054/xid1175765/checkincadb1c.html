Check In command

Checks a document into Teamcenter and makes it available to other users. If you check in a direct document, Solid Edge checks in all associated indirect documents that are checked out. In a Teamcenter-managed environment, the Check In command acts on the direct document when no document is selected, or on all documents within a selection set.

Note:

If you upload a document, you will need to use the Check In command to make it available to other users.

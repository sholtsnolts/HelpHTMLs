Go offline after exiting Solid Edge (SEEC)


If you need to work offline after you have exited Solid Edge, but you did not select Work Offline from the Cache Assistant dialog box before exiting:

  1. Log in to Solid Edge, and click the File tab.

  2. Click Settings→Options→Manage→Login tab.

  3. Click Go Offline .

  4. Click OK.

Construct an etch

  1. Choose the Home tab→Sheet Metal group→Dimple command list→Etch button .

  2. Click the text profiles or sketch elements to create the etch.

  3. (Optional) Click the Options button to display the Etch Options dialog box to define various etch attributes.

  4. Click the Accept button .

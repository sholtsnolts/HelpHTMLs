Transform a document to a synchronous sheet metal part

  1. Choose Tools menu→Transform group→Thin Part to Synchronous Sheet Metal .

  2. (Optional) On the Thin Part to Synchronous Sheet Metal command bar, click the Options button.

  3. (Optional) Use the Thin Part to Sheet Metal Options dialog box to define information about bend relief for the part.

  4. Click the base face for the transformation.

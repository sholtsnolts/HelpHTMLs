Rip a corner of a sheet metal part

  1. Choose Home tab→Sheet Metal group→Corners list→Rip Corner.

  2. Click the corner edges you want to rip.

  3. On the command bar, click the Accept button.

  4. On the command bar, click the Preview button.

  5. On the command bar, click the Finish button.

Construct a hem

  1. Choose Home tab→Sheet Metal group→Contour Flange list→Hem .

  2. Select the edge(s) for the hem.

  3. Click to complete the hem.

Tip:

  • You can use the Hem Options dialog box to specify the type of hem to be created, along with bend radius, and flange length for the hem. The options that are available depend on the type of hem being created.

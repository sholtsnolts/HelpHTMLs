Close a 3-bend corner

  1. Choose Home tab→Sheet Metal group→Corners list→Close 3-Bend Corner .

  2. Select the first outer bend.

    Note:

    The bend angle must be less than or equal to 90 degrees.

  3. Select the second bend.

  4. Use the command bar boxes to define the characteristics of the feature.

  5. Finish the feature.

Tip:

  • You can hold the Shift key and click a highlighted bend to remove it from the select set.

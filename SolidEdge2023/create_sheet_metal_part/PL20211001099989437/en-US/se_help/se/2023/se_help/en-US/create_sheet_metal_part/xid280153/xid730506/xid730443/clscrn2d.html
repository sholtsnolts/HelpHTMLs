Close 2-Bend Corner command bar

Closed Corner

Closes the selected corners.

Overlapping Corners

Overlaps the selected corners.

Flip

Reverses the overlapping of the flanges. This option is only available for overlapping corners.

Treatment

Specifies the treatment you want to apply to the bent faces of the flanges. You can apply a close treatment,

open treatment,

circular cutout treatment,

u-shaped treatment,

v-shaped treatment,

square treatment,

or miter treatment to the bent faces.

The Close and Circular Cutout treatment options are supported in 2-bend corners where the bend angles are not equal.

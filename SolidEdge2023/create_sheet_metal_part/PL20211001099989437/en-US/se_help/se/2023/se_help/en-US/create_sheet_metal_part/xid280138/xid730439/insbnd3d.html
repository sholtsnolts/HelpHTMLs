Bend command bar

Bend Options

Displays the Bend Options dialog box so you can set the bend construction options.

Material Side
Center of Mold Lines

Positions the bend by placing the moving side of the material centered about the mode line.

Mold Lines

Positions the bend by placing the moving side of the material with respect to the left or right of the mold line.

Material Inside

Positions the bend by placing the moving side of the material inside the sketch geometry.

Material Outside

Positions the bend by placing the moving side of the material outside the sketch geometry.

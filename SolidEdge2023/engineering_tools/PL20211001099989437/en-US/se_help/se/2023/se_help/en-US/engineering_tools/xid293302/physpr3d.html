Principal Tab

Displays the principal physical properties of a part or assembly.

Orientation Of Principal Axes

Displays the direction cosines of the principal axes with respect to the defined origin (the specified coordinate system) and directions.

  • x

  • y

  • z

  • 2x

  • 2y

  • 2z

  • 3x

  • 3y

  • 3z

Display Symbol

Displays the principal axes symbols in the window.

Principal Moments Of Inertia

Displays the principal moments of inertia with respect to the principal axes.

  • I1: Moment of inertia of mass about the first principal axis.

  • I2: Moment of inertia of mass about the second principal axis.

  • I3: Moment of inertia of mass about the third principal axis.

Radii Of Gyration

Displays the radii of gyration with respect to the principal axes.

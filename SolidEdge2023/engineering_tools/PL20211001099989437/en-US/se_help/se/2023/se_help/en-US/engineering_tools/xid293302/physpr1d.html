Global Tab

Displays the global physical properties of a part, sheet metal part, or an assembly.

Mass

Displays the mass of the part or assembly in the active units for mass.

Volume

Displays the volume of the part or assembly in the active working units for volume.

Surface Area

Displays the surface area for the part in the active working units for area. This option is available only in the Part and Sheet Metal environments.

Center Of Mass

Displays the coordinates for the center of mass with respect to the defined origin (coordinate system) of the part's, weldment's, or assembly's design space.

  • X: x coordinate

  • Y: y coordinate

  • Z: z coordinate

Display Symbol

Displays the center of mass symbol in the window.

Center Of Volume

Displays the coordinates for the center of volume with respect to the defined origin (coordinate system) of the part's, weldment's, or assembly's design space.

Display Symbol

Displays the center of volume symbol in the window.

Mass Moments Of Inertia

Displays the six principal mass moments of inertia with respect to the defined origin (coordinate system) of the part's, weldment's, or assembly's design space.

  • Ixx: Moment of inertia of mass about x axis.

  • Ixy: Product of inertia of mass with respect to x and y axes.

  • Ixz: Product of inertia of mass with respect to x and z axes.

  • Iyy: Moment of inertia of mass about y axis.

  • Izz: Moment of inertia of mass about z axis.

  • Iyz: Product of inertia of mass with respect to y and z axes.

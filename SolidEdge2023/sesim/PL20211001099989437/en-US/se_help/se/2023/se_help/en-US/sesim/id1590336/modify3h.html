Activate a study

You have to activate a study before you can modify it. Here are two ways you can do this.

From the ribbon:

  • On the ribbon, in the Study group, select the name from the Study Name list.

From the Simulation pane:

  1. Click the Simulation tab.

  2. On the Simulation pane, click the name of the study you want to activate, such as Static Study 1, Modal Study 1, or Buckling Study 1.

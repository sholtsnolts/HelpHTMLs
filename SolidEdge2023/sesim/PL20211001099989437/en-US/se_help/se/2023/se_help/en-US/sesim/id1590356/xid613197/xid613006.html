How overriding properties affects design optimization

Physical properties are not available as a design objective or a design limit in study optimization when:

  • In a study of surface geometry, you change the material or the thickness using the Override Property command.

  • In a study of an assembly, in the Edit Material dialog box, you clear the Use material from the occurrence check box.

  • In a study using a sheet metal mid-surface, in the Edit Thickness dialog box, you clear the Use thickness from sheet metal check box.

  • A study uses the Surface mesh type or General Bodies mesh type.

  • The simulation geometry in an assembly study uses the default assembly material, because the material in individual parts is not defined.

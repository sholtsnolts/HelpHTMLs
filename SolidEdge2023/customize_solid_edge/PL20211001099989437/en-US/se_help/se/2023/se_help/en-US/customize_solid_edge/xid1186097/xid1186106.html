Restore Solid Edge settings and preferences

You can restore the settings and preferences that you previously deployed, using the Settings and Preferences Wizard. You can also restore them programmatically, as explained in Manage settings and preferences programmatically.

  1. When Solid Edge is not running, choose Start→Siemens Solid Edge 2023→Solid Edge Tools→Settings and Preferences Wizard.

  2. Select Restore Solid Edge factory Settings and Preferences.

  3. Click Finish.

Customize the status bar

Commands and tools that are located on the status bar by default can be hidden and redisplayed.

Turn status bar commands on and off

  1. Position the cursor on the status bar at the bottom of the application window, and right-click.

  2. On the Customize Status Bar menu, do one of the following:

    • Clear the check mark in front of items you want to hide.

    • Set the check mark in front of items you want to show.

    The user interface is updated when you close the menu.

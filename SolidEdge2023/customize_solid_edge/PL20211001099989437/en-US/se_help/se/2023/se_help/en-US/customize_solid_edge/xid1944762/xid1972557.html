Delete a rule in Dynamic Visualization

  1. Open a Solid Edge assembly.

  2. From the Inspect tab→Report group, click Dynamic Visualization.

    The rules defined for the open assembly are displayed in the Dynamic Visualization dialog box.

  3. Select the rule you want to delete.

  4. From the list of commands on the right, click Show More , and then click Delete Rule .

  5. Confirm that you want to delete the selected rule.

    The rule is deleted from the list of rules for the open assembly.

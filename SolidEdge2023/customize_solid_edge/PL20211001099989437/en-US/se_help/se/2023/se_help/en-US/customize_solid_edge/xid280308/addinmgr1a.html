Add-Ins

Add-Ins are external programs created by a variety of vendors that extend the capabilities of Solid Edge for a specific workflow, industry, or application. Registered Solid Edge add-ins can be loaded and unloaded by means of the Add-In Manager, which also displays information about the add-in and provides access to an add-in's on-line help file.

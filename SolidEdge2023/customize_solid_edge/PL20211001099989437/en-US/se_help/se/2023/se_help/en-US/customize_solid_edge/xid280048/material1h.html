Apply a material to a part

  1. Right-click the Material node in PathFinder and choose Material Table, or choose File menu→Info→Material Table.

  2. In the Solid Edge Material Table dialog box, select the material name you want from the left pane, and then click Apply To Model.

Tip:

  • The material names and property sets are stored in an external file with a .mtl, located in the C:\Program Files\Siemens\Solid Edge 2023\Preferences\Materials folder.

  • The material and mechanical properties associated with the material, such as face style, fill style, density, and coefficient of thermal expansion, as well as other default and custom properties, are applied to the part model.

  • The material and mechanical properties are maintained when you place the part in an assembly, render the assembly, create a parts list on a drawing, and define a bill of materials.

Copying and pasting materials

You can copy materials or directories and then paste them in any location in the left pane of the Material Table. If a copied material or directory has a duplicate name, the original name is maintained and a unique number is added to it.

Material: Copper → Copper (1)

Directory: Steel → Steel (1)

Note:

When you copy and paste a directory, the materials in the directory receive unique names if they are duplicated.

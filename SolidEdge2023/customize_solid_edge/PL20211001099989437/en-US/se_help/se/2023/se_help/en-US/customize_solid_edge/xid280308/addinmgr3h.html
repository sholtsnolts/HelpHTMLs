Unload a registered Add-In

  1. On the File menu, click Settings→Add-Ins.

  2. In the Available Add-Ins box, click to remove the check mark in the box to the left of the add-in you want to unload.

    Add-ins that are to be unloaded do not have a check mark in this box.

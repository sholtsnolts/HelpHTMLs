Unsuppress an assembly relationship

  1. Click the PathFinder tab.

  2. In the top pane of PathFinder, select the part which you want to unsuppress a relationship.

    Note:

    The part highlights in the assembly window and the bottom pane of PathFinder shows the relationships applied to the part you selected.

  3. In the bottom pane of PathFinder, click the relationship you want to unsuppress.

    Note:

    The faces that were used to apply the relationship highlight in the assembly window to show which relationship will be unsuppressed.

  4. Right-click to display the shortcut menu, then click Unsuppress.

Tip:

When you unsuppress an assembly relationship, the relationship symbol in the bottom pane of PathFinder changes color.

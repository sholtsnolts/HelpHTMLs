Select a feature with PathFinder

  1. In PathFinder, click the Feature Library tab.

  2. In the Feature Library, highlight the feature you want to select by positioning the cursor over its name in PathFinder.

    PathFinder highlights all part features, even those that are no longer visible because they were consumed by other features.

To clear a selection

  • Do one of the following:

    • Click in free space.

    • Click the right mouse button in free space.

    • Select another feature.

Tip:

  • To select more than one feature, hold down the Ctrl key, and then click the features you want.

  • To select a list of features, click on the first feature in the list, hold down the Shift key, and then click the last feature in the list.

Move an ordered feature to synchronous

  1. In ordered modeling, right-click on the ordered feature to move to the synchronous body.

    Note:

    All ordered features listed in PathFinder above the selected ordered feature are moved to the synchronous node.

  2. On the shortcut menu, choose the Move to Synchronous command.
